Hi, I'm Andy, a Gold Coast based freelance photographer and videographer with a genuine passion for creating visuals that mean something. From a young age, photography became more than just a hobby; it became a way of seeing the world and the people in it. That same curiosity and care drives every shoot I take on today.

My approach is built on collaboration and intention. Whether you're a brand looking to elevate your identity or an individual wanting to feel confident in front of the camera, I take the time to understand what you need and craft visuals that truly reflect it. Every shot is considered, every detail matters.

Beyond client work, I'm constantly exploring new styles, techniques, and creative directions, not just to sharpen my skills, but because I genuinely love the craft. That passion shows up in the work, and it's what keeps every project feeling fresh, purposeful, and uniquely yours.

I create refined visual content that captures attention while staying true to the people and brands behind it. Each project is approached with care, combining thoughtful direction with natural, engaging storytelling.
